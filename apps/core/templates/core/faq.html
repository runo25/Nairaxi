What is car leasing?

{# UPDATED #} {# UPDATED #}
{# UPDATED #}

Car leasing is a long-term rental agreement where you pay a fixed monthly fee to use a vehicle for a specified period (usually 1-3 years), instead of buying it outright. At the end of the term, you simply return the car.

{# UPDATED #}

Who is eligible to lease a car from Fleet?

{# UPDATED #} {# UPDATED #}
{# UPDATED #}

To lease a car with us, you must be at least 21 years old, possess a valid Nigerian driver's license, and meet our credit and verification requirements. Specific requirements may vary by vehicle.

{# UPDATED #} {# UPDATED #}
