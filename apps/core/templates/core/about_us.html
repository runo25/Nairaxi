Our Story

Redefining Mobility in Nigeria

Nairaxi was founded with a clear vision: to solve the complex mobility challenges faced by individuals and businesses in Nigeria. We saw a need for a reliable, flexible, and trustworthy car leasing service that prioritized customer experience above all else.

Starting with a small, dedicated team and a handful of vehicles, we've grown by focusing on a simple principle: providing high-quality cars with a seamless, transparent process. From ride-hailing to premium long-term leasing, our journey is fueled by a commitment to innovation and your satisfaction.
